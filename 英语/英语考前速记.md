## 作文

**邮件格式**

```
Dear xxx,(逗号是必须的)
Hello xxx,(不熟就用这个)

## 开头说明写信的目的
I hope this email finds you well(我希望这封邮件能找到你). I am writing this email to ...

Thank you for taking the time read this email(感谢您在百忙之中阅读这封邮件). I am writing this email to ...

## 请求类
I was wondering if you could(我在想你能不能) ...
Would it be possible to(有可能去) ... 

## 道歉类
I apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused(对于由此造成的任何不便，我深表歉意).
I am sorry for any confusion(很抱歉给您造成了任何困惑).
Please accept my apologies for(请接受我的道歉)...

### 请求帮助类
Thank you in advance for your help(提前感谢你的帮助).
I appreciate your assistance(谢谢你的帮助).
You help is greatly appreciated(非常感谢你的帮助).

### 结尾
Please let me know if you have any further questions(如果你还有什么问题，请告诉我).
I look forward to hearing from you soon(我期待很快收到你的来信).
Thank you again for your time and attention(再次感谢你的时间和关注).


Best regards,(逗号是必须的)
xxxx(你的名字)
```
